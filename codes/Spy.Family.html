ABOUT Spy Family

Spy Family is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Tatsuya Endo. The story follows a spy who must create a fake family in order to complete a mission, but the twist is that the daughter he adopts is a telepath and the wife he marries is an assassin. The series has been well-received for its unique blend of comedy, action, and heartwarming moments.

CHARACTERS

  • Loid Forger: The main protagonist, a skilled spy who takes on the identity of a family man to complete his mission.
  • Also Known as
  • Twilight: Loid's code name as a spy, known for his exceptional skills and ability to adapt to any situation.
  • Yor Forger: Loid's wife, who is secretly an assassin. She is kind-hearted and protective of her family.
  • Anya Forger: The adopted daughter of Loid and Yor, who has telepathic abilities. She is cheerful and loves her new family.
