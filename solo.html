ABOUT SOLO LEVELING

Solo Leveling is a South Korean web novel written by Chugong. The story follows Sung Jin-Woo, a weak and low-ranked hunter who suddenly gains the ability to level up and become stronger after surviving a dangerous dungeon. As he embarks on his journey to become the strongest hunter, he faces various challenges and battles against powerful monsters. The series has gained immense popularity for its unique storyline, character development, and intense action scenes.

CHARACTERS

  • Sung Jin-Woo: The protagonist of the series, a weak hunter who becomes stronger after gaining the ability to level up.
  • Cha Hae-In: A skilled hunter and member of the Hunter's Association who becomes an ally to Sung Jin-Woo.
  • Go Gun-Hee: The chairman of the Hunter's Association who recognizes Sung Jin-Woo's potential and supports him.
  • Yoo Jin-Ho: A loyal friend and fellow hunter who accompanies Sung Jin-Woo on his journey.
